# Bugs of Planet Dune
A Unity‑built 3D action shooter set on a harsh desert world filled with giant insects and hostile alien creatures. You must defend your outpost from waves of oversized mosquitoes, scorpions, and worms, collect gate keys, and escape before the swarm overwhelms you.

## Gameplay Overview 🚀
Bugs of Planet Dune takes place in a sandy, dune‑covered environment filled with strange fungus, abandoned structures, and locked gates. As the player, you explore the desert while fending off aggressive insects that attack your base. Your objective is to survive the onslaught, locate scattered gate keys, unlock the exit, and escape the planet alive. The game blends exploration, shooting mechanics, and light puzzle‑solving in a hostile alien landscape.

## Controls 🎮
W / A / S / D — Move
Mouse Cursor — Aim
Left Mouse Button — Shoot
Escape Button — Pause the game

## Hint 💡
Search for gate keys inside buildings or near locked gates. You’ll need them to progress through the desert outpost.

## Features ✨
Bugs of Planet Dune includes a variety of mechanics designed to create a tense and action‑focused experience. Players can explore a desert‑themed world with sci‑fi elements, fight off waves of giant insects using ranged weapons, and search for gate keys hidden throughout the environment. The combination of shooting, exploration, and survival elements creates a fast‑paced gameplay loop where staying alert is essential.
